About Us.

origins

We take pride in the knowledge that our skills have been passed down through the generations. As my dad used to say, you only have one chance to make a first impression.

My dad, Jimmy started building in Port Townsend and the Jefferson County area in the 1960's and passed his experience and knowledge down to the next generation. He believed that hard work and integrity defined a person and a business. His ideas continue on.

our philosophy

Build it right the first time, as the saying goes. We make a point to use the proper lay-up and waterproofing techniques to ensure the longevity of your home. Protecting your investment is a one of our highest priorities. We take extra care to build energy efficient homes.
